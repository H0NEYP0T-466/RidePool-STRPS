# User Guide

Welcome to RidePool STRPS! This guide will help you understand how to use the application as a rider, driver, or administrator.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

1. Go to the RidePool homepage
2. Click **Register** in the top right
3. Choose your role:
   - **Rider** - To request and share rides
   - **Driver** - To accept and complete rides
4. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
5. Click **Create Account**

### 2. Log In

1. Go to the **Login** page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

You'll be redirected to your dashboard based on your role.

---

## For Riders

### Booking a Ride

1. From your dashboard, click **Book a Ride**
2. On the map:
   - Click to set your **pickup location** (green marker)
   - Click to set your **dropoff location** (red marker)
3. Enable **Pooling** to share your ride and save up to 25%
4. Review the estimated fare
5. Click **Request Ride**

### Tracking Your Ride

Once a driver accepts your ride:

1. Go to **Track Ride** from the navigation
2. See your driver's real-time location on the map
3. View driver details:
   - Name
   - Vehicle type and number
   - Rating
4. Track the ride status:
   - **Matched** - Driver assigned
   - **In Progress** - Trip started
   - **Completed** - Trip finished

### Viewing Ride History

1. Click **History** in the navigation
2. See all your past rides
3. Filter by status:
   - All
   - Requested
   - Completed
   - Cancelled
4. View ride details including:
   - Pickup/dropoff locations
   - Fare
   - Date and time
   - Pooling status

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- Total rides taken
- Completed rides
- Total amount spent
- Amount saved through pooling
- Recent ride activity
- Quick action buttons

## For Drivers

### Setting Availability

1. On your dashboard, toggle the **Online/Offline** switch
2. When online, you'll receive ride requests
3. Turn offline when you're not available

### Accepting Rides

1. Go to **Ride Requests** to see pending requests
2. Each request shows:
   - Passenger name
   - Pickup location
   - Dropoff location
   - Estimated fare
   - Whether pooling is enabled
3. Click **Accept Ride** to take the request
4. Click **Decline** to skip

### Managing Active Rides

1. Go to **Active Ride** after accepting
2. Use the status buttons to update:
   - **Start Trip** - When you pick up the passenger
   - **Complete Trip** - When you drop them off
   - **Cancel Ride** - If ride cannot be completed
3. The passenger will receive real-time updates

### Route Optimization

For pooled rides with multiple passengers:

1. Go to **Route** in the navigation
2. See the optimized route with numbered waypoints
3. View total distance and estimated time
4. Follow the waypoints in order

### Viewing Earnings

Your dashboard shows:
- Today's earnings
- Weekly earnings
- Total earnings
- Rating
- Total trips completed

## Features Explained

### Ride Pooling

Pooling allows multiple passengers going in similar directions to share a ride:

- **Savings**: Up to 25-30% off the regular fare
- **How it works**:
  1. Enable pooling when booking
  2. System finds matches going your way
  3. Driver picks up multiple passengers
  4. Each pays a reduced fare

### Real-time Tracking

- Driver locations update every few seconds
- See the driver moving on the map
- Get notified when:
  - Ride is accepted
  - Driver is arriving
  - Trip starts
  - Trip completes

### Rating System

After completing a ride:
- Rate your driver (1-5 stars)
- Leave an optional comment
- Ratings help maintain service quality

### Fare Calculation

Fare is calculated as:
```
Base Fare: PKR 50
Distance Rate: PKR 15/km

Total = Base Fare + (Distance Ã— Rate)
```
